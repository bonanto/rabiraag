# 🛡️ Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 17, 2026

Our privacy philosophy is simple: **We do not want your data.** Rabiraag Keyboard is built on a strict "zero-bloat" and "zero-tracking" principle. The software is completely free to use, with no strings attached. 

### 1. Data Collection within the App
* The Rabiraag Keyboard software collects **absolutely no user data, telemetry, or typing history.** * The application operates entirely offline on your local machine and does not communicate with any external servers.

### 2. Data Collected via Communication
If you choose to contact us directly via email (`bonanto18@protonmail.com`) or interact with our public GitHub repository, we may temporarily retain the information you voluntarily provide (such as your name, email address, or GitHub username). 
* **How we use it:** This information is strictly used to reply to your inquiry, track bug fixes, or properly credit you for feature suggestions in future development updates.
* **What we do not do:** We will never sell, rent, or share your contact information with any third party for any reason. 

### 3. Website Analytics
This website does not employ aggressive tracking scripts or marketing cookies. Your visit remains your own business.
